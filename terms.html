1. Rental Agreement

By renting a vehicle from Rafal Car, you agree to comply with all terms and conditions set forth in this agreement. This agreement is between Rafal Car ("Company") and the person identified as the renter ("You" or "Renter").

2. Eligibility Requirements

To rent a vehicle from Rafal Car, you must:

  • Be at least 21 years of age (25 for luxury vehicles)
  • Hold a valid driver's license for at least 2 years
  • Provide a valid passport or national ID card
  • Have a valid credit or debit card in your name
  • Accept all terms and conditions of the rental agreement

3. Vehicle Use

3.1 Permitted Use

The rental vehicle may only be used for lawful purposes and on paved roads in Morocco. The vehicle must not be used for:

  • Racing, testing, or any competitive driving
  • Transporting illegal goods or substances
  • Subletting or lending to third parties
  • Towing or pushing other vehicles
  • Off-road driving
  • Transporting more passengers than the vehicle's capacity

3.2 Driver Responsibilities

The renter agrees to:

  • Operate the vehicle safely and in accordance with Moroccan traffic laws
  • Check oil, water, and tire pressure regularly
  • Keep the vehicle locked when unattended
  • Report any accidents or damage immediately
  • Return the vehicle on time and in the same condition

4. Rental Period and Charges

4.1 Rental Duration

The rental period begins when you take possession of the vehicle and ends when you return it to our designated location. Any extension must be authorized by Rafal Car in advance.

4.2 Rates and Fees

  • Daily rental rates as quoted at time of reservation
  • Additional driver fee: 50 MAD per day per driver
  • GPS rental: 30 MAD per day (if applicable)
  • Child seat rental: 40 MAD per day
  • Late return fee: 100 MAD per hour
  • Fuel: Vehicle must be returned with same fuel level or refueling charges apply

4.3 Security Deposit

A security deposit is required at the time of rental. The amount varies by vehicle category:

  • Economy cars: No deposit or up to 5,000 MAD
  • SUV vehicles: 10,000 MAD
  • Luxury vehicles: 20,000 MAD

The deposit will be refunded within 7-14 business days after the vehicle is returned, subject to inspection and any deductions for damages or violations.

5. Insurance and Liability

5.1 Basic Insurance

All rentals include basic insurance coverage (TPL - Third Party Liability) as required by Moroccan law.

5.2 Additional Coverage Options

  • CDW (Collision Damage Waiver): Reduces your liability for damage to the rental vehicle
  • Super CDW: Further reduces deductible to zero for most damages
  • PAI (Personal Accident Insurance): Provides medical coverage for injuries

5.3 Renter's Liability

The renter is responsible for:

  • All damage to the vehicle, regardless of fault, unless CDW/Super CDW is purchased
  • Theft of the vehicle or its parts
  • Damage caused by violations of this agreement
  • Traffic fines and parking violations
  • Towing and impound fees

6. Vehicle Return

The vehicle must be returned to the agreed location by the date and time specified in your rental agreement. You must:

  • Return the vehicle with the same fuel level
  • Return all documents, keys, and accessories
  • Clean the interior (excessive dirt may incur cleaning fees)
  • Report any damage or incidents that occurred during rental

6.1 Late Return

If you fail to return the vehicle on time without prior authorization, you will be charged for the additional time plus a late fee of 100 MAD per hour. If the vehicle is not returned within 24 hours of the due date without communication, it may be reported as stolen.

7. Accidents and Breakdowns

7.1 In Case of Accident

If you are involved in an accident, you must:

  • Contact local authorities immediately (police report required)
  • Notify Rafal Car within 2 hours: +212 662 186 949
  • Complete an accident report form
  • Obtain details from other parties involved
  • Take photos of the damage and accident scene
  • Not admit liability or sign any documents without Rafal Car's approval

7.2 Mechanical Breakdown

If the vehicle breaks down due to mechanical failure (not caused by misuse), contact us immediately for roadside assistance. Do not attempt repairs without authorization.

8. Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation more than 48 hours before pickup: Full refund
  • Cancellation 24-48 hours before pickup: 50% refund
  • Cancellation less than 24 hours before pickup: No refund
  • No-show: Full charge applies
