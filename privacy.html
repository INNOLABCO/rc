Rafal Car - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Identification documents (driver's license, passport)
  • Rental details (dates, locations, vehicle preferences)
  • Payment information

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process vehicle reservations and rentals
  • Verify driver qualifications
  • Comply with Moroccan legal requirements
  • Communicate about your rental
  • Improve our services

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for the duration required by Moroccan law (typically 7 years for rental agreements) and as necessary for business purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

6. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • As required by Moroccan law
  • With insurance providers when necessary
  • With law enforcement if legally required

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
